Green cacti are succulent plants with thick, water-storing stems and spines. They're native to arid regions and come in various shapes and sizes. They thrive in bright sunlight, require infrequent watering, and prefer well-draining soil. These low-maintenance plants add a touch of nature to indoor spaces.
